# MUMOSA Project Brief Notes

## Client
- DEVCOM Army Research Laboratory
- MUMOSA (MUlti-MOdal Situation Awareness)

## Problem Space
MUMOSA is an interactive dashboard for making sense of complex crisis events by connecting text reports, images, structured event information, schema graphs, and 3D/simulation views.

## Intended Users
- Investigators
- Analysts
- Emergency responders

## Immediate Course Deliverables
- Individual literature review
- Group heuristics / interaction-design presentation
- Paper prototype and testing
- Electronic prototype and testing
- Final client-facing report and presentation

## Current Team Direction
The present concept work is especially focused on the VR section: using reconstructed scenes as a safe, post-crisis review surface for evidence exploration, training, and spatial understanding.
